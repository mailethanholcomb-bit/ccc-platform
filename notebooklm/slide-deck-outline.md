## Slide 5: The DSCR Deep Dive
**DSCR = SDE / Annual Debt Service**
- 1.80x+ = Grade A (excellent)
- 1.50x+ = Grade B (strong)
- 1.25x+ = Grade C (bank minimum)
- 1.00x+ = Grade D (marginal)
- Below 1.00x = Grade F (deal killer)

Visual: Gauge chart showing DSCR zones

## Slide 11: Engine 5 — Verdict Engine
**Three-Phase Decision Cascade (First Match Wins)**

Phase 1 — Hard NO-GO:
- DSCR < 1.25x in all scenarios
- Revenue down > 30% YoY
- Grade F | 4+ FAIL scores | SDE < $100K

Phase 2 — CONDITIONAL:
- DSCR between 1.25x and member floor
- Grade D | 2-3 FAIL scores | 1-2 critical red flags

Phase 3 — GO (Default):
- All checks passed, no disqualifiers

## Slide 12: Engine 6 — Email Generator
**Two Options for Every Decline:**
- Option A (Clean Pass): Definitive, professional decline
- Option B (Warm Open): Soft decline, invites counter-offer

Auto-selects based on severity:
- 3+ critical flags → Option A
- Fewer critical flags → Option B
- CONDITIONAL → Always Option B
